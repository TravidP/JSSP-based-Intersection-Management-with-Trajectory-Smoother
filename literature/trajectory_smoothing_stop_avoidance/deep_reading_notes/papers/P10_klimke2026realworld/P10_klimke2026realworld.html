1. 论文全景速览与核心贡献 / High-Level Overview & Core Contributions

研究背景与痛点 / Background & Pain Points

这篇论文位于“Real-world cooperative intersection management”方向。对你的 JSSP-based Intersection Management 课题，它回答的是高层调度、低层轨迹平滑、安全过滤或真实验证中的一个关键子问题：如何让车辆在路口少停、少冲突、少能耗，同时保持在线可执行。

The paper belongs to the theme of Real-world cooperative intersection management. For a JSSP-based intersection-management thesis, it illuminates one of the key layers: scheduling, smoothing, safety filtering, or real-world validation.

Focuses on cooperative maneuver planning evaluation, not a JSSP formulation.

核心科学问题 / Core Scientific Question

协同交叉口管理方法在混合交通仿真与真实原型车测试中是否仍能减少停车和通行时间？

Do cooperative intersection-management approaches reduce stops and crossing time in both mixed-traffic simulation and real prototype drives?

科学贡献 / Scientific Contributions

  • 方法贡献 (method contribution)：Compares multi-scenario prediction and graph-based reinforcement learning approaches in mixed-traffic simulation and real drives.
  • 安全/避碰贡献 (safety contribution)：Evaluates criticality metrics while operating with prototype connected automated vehicles in public traffic.
  • 平滑/停止避免贡献 (smoothing contribution)：Reports substantial reductions in crossing time and number of stops for cooperative maneuver planning.
  • 创新力度评判 (reviewer judgement)：强验证价值：真实道路评估稀缺，是论文实验设计和指标选择的优质参考。

核心算法架构 / Core Algorithm Layout

论文不是提出单一新控制算法，而是比较多场景预测和图强化学习等协同机动规划模块，在混合交通仿真与真实车辆中评估停车、时间和关键性。

Rather than only proposing a controller, the paper evaluates cooperative maneuver-planning modules in simulation and real-world prototype drives.

收敛性、稳定性或实时性 / Convergence, Stability, Real-Time Feasibility

贡献在验证闭环：真实车辆实验暴露通信、定位、感知和人类交通干扰。它为仿真论文提供“哪些指标必须走向真实”的标尺。

Its value is validation realism: real drives reveal communication, localization, perception, and mixed-traffic issues hidden in pure simulation.

潜在假设与边界条件 / Assumptions & Boundary Conditions

  • 原型系统中的通信与感知可支持协同机动规划。
  • 真实测试场景代表性足以支持初步结论。
  • 关键性指标可近似反映真实安全风险。

Novel mixed-traffic simulation framework plus real-world prototype connected automated vehicle drives.

核心指标 / Metrics

4. 审稿人视角：批判性思考与未来方向 / Critical Thinking & Future Directions

论文局限性 / Limitations & Weaknesses

  • 样本规模和场景覆盖通常小于大规模仿真。
  • 不是 JSSP 建模论文，理论调度贡献有限。
  • 真实测试条件可能较温和，极端场景不足。

博士课题拓展点 / Research Extensions for PhD

  • 把你的 JSSP scheduler 输出转化为相同停车/穿越时间/关键性指标，形成可对标实验。
  • 设计 sim-to-real gap 表格：仿真中通过的策略在真实系统中哪些环节会失效。
  • 加入通信延迟、定位噪声和 HDV 插入，复现实验鲁棒性。
如何服务当前课题 / Use in This Project

Excellent evidence that stop count is a practical metric and that real-world mixed traffic should be considered in future validation.
